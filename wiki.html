Wiki

The wiki is a useful source for information about Sim Lore, members, and many other weird and wonderful things. However, it is an undiscovered resource to be tapped into. Various commanders and webmasters over the history of Sapcesim, each with their own system of organization, have added valuable knowledge over the years. The result, lots of interesting and valuable information with no system to document and organize it.
